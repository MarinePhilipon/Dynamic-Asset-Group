How do you want to manage creatives for this strategy?

This setting determines how Rokt Brain will optimise your creative assets within this Ad Set. You can change this later.

Recommended

⚡ Dynamic (Asset Group)

Upload assets once. Rokt Brain assembles and tests combinations across all eligible formats at the impression level.

  • Multi-format reach from a single upload
  • Component-level optimisation by Brain
  • Auto-eligible for new formats on launch
  • Asset-level performance reporting
Median 19.5% uplift vs single-format baseline in Rokt's network

🎛 Manual (Fixed format)

Full control over layout and composition. Brain does not vary asset combinations. Choose the format you want, set it, ship it.

  • Fixed format rendering
  • Exact brand and copy control
  • Required for regulated verticals
  • Controlled A/B tests with direct attribution
