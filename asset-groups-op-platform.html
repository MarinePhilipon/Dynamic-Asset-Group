How do you want to create this creative?

Choose Dynamic Asset Groups to let Rokt Brain optimise combinations across all eligible formats automatically — or create a Manual Creative for full control over format and composition.

⚡

Dynamic Asset Group

Upload a pool of assets once. Brain assembles and tests all combinations across every eligible format at the impression level — no manual format wiring needed.

  • Auto-eligible for all Rokt formats based on assets provided
  • Brain optimises headline + image + CTA combos per impression
  • Component-level reporting shows which assets drive results
  • New formats automatically inherit your existing assets
  • Best for scale, performance and ML-driven learning
19.5% median CVR uplift vs single-format creatives
🎛

Manual Creative

Choose a specific format and control exactly how your ad renders. Creatives are locked to one format. Best for brand-sensitive campaigns or precision testing.

Text Benefits Savings Text Hero Image Compact Product Carousel
  • Fixed format — exactly what you design is what renders
  • Full brand and copy control per format
  • Essential for regulated verticals
  • Useful for controlled A/B tests with direct attribution
  • Requires re-authoring for each format separately
